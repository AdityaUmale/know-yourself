Title: Big Five Personality Traits

1. Openness to Experience: Creativity, curiosity, imagination.
2. Conscientiousness: Organization, responsibility, reliability.
3. Extraversion: Sociability, assertiveness, enthusiasm.
4. Agreeableness: Compassion, cooperativeness, kindness.
5. Neuroticism: Emotional instability, anxiety, moodiness.

These traits help understand one's personality and behavior patterns.
